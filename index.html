Our Services

Farmicon is a single click platform where farmers can get the guidance, market reach, all the essential services for crops and veggies, solutions for crop disease using the Mobility Integrated Artificial Intelligence model, weather information to save the crops, account management etc.

Crop Price

Know the rate of your crops in the nearby marketplace. Select the crops and get the price details of your nearby market.

Crop Doctor

Just click a picture of your crop, and post it on our site to get a diagnosis of the infected crop and its solution.

Drone Spray

Spray insecticides and pesticides through drones on crops to protect them from pests and diseases and increase your yields. It is a highly efficient, safe and water-saving technique.

Crop Health Monitoring

Keep a real time check on various data parameters like nutrient effects,size of grains etc. to ensure maximum yield and quality produce.

SOIL TESTING

Get connected to your nearby soil testing lab. It measures the soil's mineral content, pH level, soil moisture, salinity etc. and tell the crop growth condition.
